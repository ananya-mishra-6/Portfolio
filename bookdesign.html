Monochrome Photography





The cover page is a minimalist approach to highlight the style of monochrome photography. Red is used with black and white, since it symbolizes power, strong emotions and need of urgency. My choice of photography includes - landscape photographs, photographs that transfer certain deep thoughts and emotions or have a social message. The layout of the book throughout is modern and minimalist. Though black and white photography was more prominent in the olden times, the meaning that they hold is equally relevant in today's times. To show the liveliness contained in the monochrome photographs I have added drop shadow to all images thereby creating emphasis on the images. The selection of font is such that is it legible and manages to incorporate the look of older times.

Time. What is it?




Medium- Chalk on black paper
The above book is visual narrative, exploring the mystery of time. The color scheme is neutral- black and white to align with the theme of mystery.
The scene depicted is a quite common scenario, wherein an office employee gets late for his meeting. Standing in the long taxi queue, he receives a text about the meeting getting postponed. Similarly, we are blessed by time and are excused from our mistakes. However, it is the human nature to always wish for more than received, as seen ahead in the frames.
