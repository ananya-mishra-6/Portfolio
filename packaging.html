Burger Box




What?

Colorful, bold and vibrant burger packaging. The typeface designed for this packaging is heavy and crispy that fits well with a street style burger cafe, mainly targeted to the teenagers and new age children.

How?

The heavy typeface and vibrant colors reflect the bold and juicy burgers, making them attractive; as seen in a street - style cafe. The cheese melting from the sides creates an appetite appeal, tapping into the consumer's senses, thereby compelling them to buy the product. The use of repeated text “burger” creates a memorable identity and ensures that the product stays top of mind for customers.

Why?

The colors yellow and orange (warm color scheme) are usually used for the fast food chains to attract the consumer. The text- “your favorite burger served fresh” is to assure the consumers trust that the products being served is fresh and good quality, and develop a feeling of trust by knowing the consumer's favorite choices. The 3 C's or the keywords outline the product covering the main aspects the consumers search for in a burger or any other fast food- Crispy, Cheesy & Crunchy.

Soda Can




Why soda can with neoclassical?

A soda can is usually seen in pop and vibrant colors, with bold and fluid design style. While, the neoclassical art is very strict and geometrical style. Another key feature of neoclassical period is high contrast the reason I chose to go ahead with soda can design in neoclassical theme. The flavor chosen for the soda can is - pomegranate, which is designed in simple geometric shapes with rough texture. To incorporate the clean look of the neoclassical style the textual information on the front face is very minimal, to maintain the focus on the embossed fruit and design.

Why pomegranate?

Pomegranate has historical significance, often associated with classical art and mythology. Its rich, deep flavor and vibrant color can mirror the grandeur and rich palette often seen in Neoclassical art. It is symbolized with abundance and immortality in ancient cultures.
